# Contributing to Beekon

Thanks for your interest in contributing to Beekon. This file applies org-wide to
every WayQ Beekon repository that doesn't define its own `CONTRIBUTING.md`.

## Contributor License Agreement (CLA)

Beekon is offered under **two** licenses — a source-available license and a
commercial license. To keep that possible, **WayQ Technologies Pvt Ltd must hold a
clear license to every contribution.** So before your first pull request can be
merged, you'll be asked to sign our Contributor License Agreement.

- Read it here: **[CLA.md](./CLA.md)**
- When you open a pull request, an automated check posts a comment if you haven't
  signed yet. To sign, add a comment on the PR containing **exactly**:

  > I have read the CLA Document and I hereby sign the CLA

- You only sign **once** — the signature applies across all Beekon repositories.
- The CLA grants WayQ a broad license to relicense your contribution (including
  under commercial terms); **you keep ownership of your copyright.**

If you're contributing as part of your employment, make sure you have your
employer's permission (see the CLA for details).

## Pull requests

- Keep PRs focused and small where possible.
- Make sure existing CI (build, lint, tests) passes.
- Describe what changed and why in the PR description.
